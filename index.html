Assessment Summary

AMTS - Abbreviated Mental Test Score

  1. What is your age?
  2. What is the time to the nearest hour?
  3. Repeat address for recall (e.g. "42 West Street")
  4. What is the current year?
  5. Where are we now (place name or house number)?
  6. Recognise two people (e.g. family or carers)
  7. What is your date of birth?
  8. In what year did WWI begin? (1914)
  9. Who is the current monarch?
  10. Count backward from 20 to 1.
